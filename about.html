About

The human behind Thrive.

I built Thrive People HR because I kept seeing the same pattern. Companies that had built something genuinely great — a product that worked, a culture people loved, a team that believed in the mission — hitting a wall the moment they needed to scale.

Not a business problem. A people infrastructure problem. The kind that doesn't show up on a P&L until it's already expensive.

After 15 years building people systems inside consumer brands, global technology companies, nonprofits, and financial services organizations — from early-stage to Fortune 500, across four continents — I realized the tools and disciplines that make large organizations resilient are rarely accessible to the companies that need them most.

Thrive changes that. Enterprise-level HR thinking, applied at your scale, without the bureaucracy.
